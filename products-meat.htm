Halal Meat & Seafood

We supply halal-certified chilled & frozen meat, plus a selection of fresh seafood. All products are handled under strict cold-chain procedures and packed to buyer specifications.

Chicken Meat

Halal fresh and frozen chicken — portioned and packed per buyer requirements. Available in bulk cartons.

Cuttlefish

Fresh frozen cuttlefish, carefully processed and IQF frozen for export.

Indian Carp

Locally sourced Indian carp — suitable for chilled or frozen shipments depending on buyer needs.

Mackerel

Good quality mackerel for retail and processing — available fresh or frozen.

Tilapia

Tilapia — farmed and handled under hygiene protocols for export markets.

Certifications & Cold Chain

All meat products can be supplied with Halal certification on request. We work with refrigerated logistics partners and provide export documentation, sanitary and phytosanitary (SPS) certificates as required by destination countries.
